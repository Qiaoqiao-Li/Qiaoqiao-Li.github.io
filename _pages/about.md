Hi there, I'm Qiaoqiao Li 👋

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Climate and Space Sciences and Engineering at the University of Michigan, working with [Prof. Mohammed Ombadi](https://ombadi.engin.umich.edu/) and [Prof. Adriana Raudzens Bailey](https://water-climate.engin.umich.edu/). My research focuses on urban greening and its role in enhancing ecosystem carbon sequestration and climate mitigation.

I obtained my PhD in Physical Geography from Beijing Normal University under the supervision of [Prof. Aizhong Ye](http://hydromodel.cn/Faculty_Students/Faculty/ye.htm). During my PhD, my research focused on drought propagation mechanisms and hydrological drought risk under the combined influences of climate change and human activities.

My research interests include:

- Ecosystem carbon sequestration and carbon cycle
- Wildfire impacts on terrestrial carbon sinks
- Hydrological processes and water resources under climate change
- Climate extremes and ecosystem resilience
- Drought propagation and drought risk assessment
- Nature-based climate solutions
